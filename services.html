Aetheria Consulting, LLC offers a comprehensive suite of cloud consulting services tailored to the unique needs of the healthcare industry. We leverage our deep understanding of healthcare workflows, regulations, and technology to deliver solutions that drive efficiency, improve patient outcomes, and ensure compliance.

Cloud Strategy & Planning

Embarking on a cloud journey requires careful planning. We work with you to develop a customized cloud strategy that aligns with your business goals, technical requirements, and budget. Our services include cloud readiness assessments, workload prioritization, and roadmap development.

Cloud Migration & Implementation

Migrating to the cloud can be complex. Our experienced team ensures a smooth and secure transition, minimizing disruption to your operations. We handle everything from data migration and application modernization to infrastructure setup and configuration.

Cloud Security & Compliance

Security and compliance are paramount in healthcare. We provide comprehensive cloud security solutions that protect your sensitive data and ensure compliance with HIPAA, HITRUST, and other relevant regulations. Our services include security assessments, vulnerability management, and incident response.

Cloud Optimization & Management

The cloud is not a "set it and forget it" solution. We provide ongoing cloud optimization and management services to ensure your cloud environment is performing at its best. Our services include cost optimization, performance monitoring, and proactive support.

Data Analytics & Insights

Unlock the power of your healthcare data with our data analytics and insights services. We help you collect, analyze, and visualize your data to gain valuable insights that improve patient care, optimize operations, and drive innovation.
